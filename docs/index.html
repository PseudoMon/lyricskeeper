PseudoMon's Personal Lyrics Keeper

Personal site by PseudoMon to keep track of the lyrics to songs she likes.

I just want a neat simple little site to keep everything, instead of having to wade through, ugh, Fandom Wiki, for some of the more obscure songs.

Made with Eleventy with the Liquid engine, and otherwise the most vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript you can ever see. Yes, including the search thing at the top right. That was a bit of effort huh.
